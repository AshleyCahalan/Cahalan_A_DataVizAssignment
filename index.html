Canada is the BEST Team at the Winter Olympics

Over the years, Canada has proven itself to be a strong competitor in the Winter Olympic Games year after year. As a Canadian myself, I may be a little biased, but I think Canada is the BEST team in the Winter Games. To help prove this opinion, Olympic sports and medals data (provided by the IOC Research and Reference Service and published by The Guardian's Datablog) for 45 countries competing between 1924 and 2014 was analyzed.
